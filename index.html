FAQ

Questions? Look here.

How does the reservation process work?

Simply explore restaurants, choose your preferred time slot, and confirm your reservation in a few clicks. You will receive an instant confirmation.

Can I add my favourite restaurant in wishlist?

Yes, simply tap the heart icon on any restaurant profile to save it to your favorites for quick access later.

How far in advance can I make a reservation?

You can book a table up to 30 days in advance depending on the restaurant's policy.

How do I upgrade to a premium membership?

Go to your profile settings and select "Membership". Follow the instructions to upgrade.

What are the payment options for premium subscriptions?

We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Is App available on both iOS and Android?

Yes, MyMeal is available for download on both the App Store and Google Play Store.
